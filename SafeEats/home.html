Welcome to Safe Eats

Safe Eats is a web application that will allow you to create an account and input the allergens that you have and search to find new foods, or to check if a food item has your allergens in it.

While every care is taken to ensure that the information is up-to-date, please be sure to double check the ingredients as manufacturer's change their recipes and we don't always catch it as soon as it happens.
